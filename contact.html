FAQ

Quick Questions

How do I reach the resort from Bharatpur Airport?

We offer complimentary airport transfers for guests who book directly with us. Simply let us know your flight details in advance.

What are the operating hours for reception?

Our reception and front desk are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Specific facilities have their own operating hours.

How do I report a maintenance issue during my stay?

Contact the front desk directly or use the in-room phone. Our maintenance team responds within 30 minutes for urgent issues.

Can I host my wedding or event at Taaza Resort?

Absolutely! We have beautiful venues for all types of events. Contact our events team for detailed packages and pricing.
