📧 Email Notifications (EmailJS)

Auto-send booking confirmation emails to guests. Free plan: 200 emails/month.

📖 How to set up EmailJS (click to expand)
  1. Go to emailjs.com and create a free account
  2. Add an Email Service (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo — connect your resort email)
  3. Create an Email Template — use the variables below in your template
  4. Copy your Public Key from Account → General
  5. Paste all three IDs in the fields below and click Save
EmailJS Template Variables to use:
{{to_email}}Guest email {{to_name}}Guest full name {{booking_id}}Booking ID {{room_type}}Room / hall type {{check_in}}Check-in date {{check_out}}Check-out date {{nights}}Number of nights {{guests_count}}Number of guests {{total_amount}}Total amount (Rs.) {{deposit_amount}}Advance deposit (Rs.) {{booking_status}}Booking status {{package_name}}Selected package {{resort_name}}Resort name {{resort_phone}}Resort phone {{resort_email}}Resort email
