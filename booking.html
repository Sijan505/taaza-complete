FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 7 days before your check-in date. Cancellations within 7 days may incur a one-night charge.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late checkout are subject to availability.

Are taxes included in the room rates?

Room rates are inclusive of all charges. No additional fees will be added during checkout.

Do you offer group bookings?

Yes! We offer special rates for group bookings of 5 rooms or more. Contact us directly for customised group quotes.

Is WiFi complimentary in all rooms?

Yes, high-speed WiFi is completely complimentary in all rooms and throughout the resort.

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 7 days before check-in without any charges. Contact our reservations team for assistance.
