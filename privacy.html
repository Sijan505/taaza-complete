Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you make a booking, place a delivery order, or contact us, we collect personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, and delivery address. We may also collect information about your visit preferences and feedback.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process reservations and delivery orders, communicate with you about your booking, send you updates and promotional offers (only if you have subscribed), and improve our services. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Booking and order information is stored locally on your device and shared directly with our team via WhatsApp or email. We do not maintain an external database of guest records.

4. Cookies

Our website uses your browser's local storage to remember your cart items and preferences. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Maps to display our location, and eSewa for payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies which govern the data they collect.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you by contacting us at tamanganish421@gmail.com.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at tamanganish421@gmail.com or call +977 9742864405.
